About Our Platform

This project is built for people who love discovering, sharing, and organizing music. Whether you're an artist posting your latest track or a listener curating your favorites, the platform gives you a clean and intuitive space to explore songs, track your activity, and interact with the community.


Our goal is simple:

make music discovery personal, fast, and enjoyable.


What We Offer


User Account Authentication
Song Posting

Upload and manage your own tracks with ease.

Likes & Activity Tracking

Like songs, revisit your recently liked tracks, and explore your listening habits.


Technology Behind the Platform

Frontend: Angular

Backend: Node.js + Express

MongoDB

We’re constantly improving the platform, adding new features, and refining the experience based on user feedback.
