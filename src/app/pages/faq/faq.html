❓ FAQ PAGE


Do I need an account to use the platform?

Yes. Creating an account allows you to post songs, like tracks, and access personalized features.


How do I upload a song?

Once logged in, go to the Add Song section, fill in the required details, and submit your track. Its going straight to Songs Catalog. It will appear in your profile too.


How do likes work

When you like a song, it’s saved to your activity history, which you can check in your profile.


Will there be a mobile app?

Not yet — but the platform is fully responsive and works well on mobile browsers. A native app may come later.

What features are coming next?


Comments for each song.
Better search and filtering
